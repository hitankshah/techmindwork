Cookie Policy.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and choices
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about website usage and performance
3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Maintaining your session and login status
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Providing relevant content and recommendations
4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device, including:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for sharing functionality
  • Content delivery networks for improved performance
5. Cookie Duration

Cookies may be:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a specified period
6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set
7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your browsing experience and limit access to certain features of our website.

8. Updates to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
